About

Abstract

Image manipulation to mislead people has been a major problem since growth in digital age. But recently the advancement in deep learning has made it easier to manipulate images/videos. Deepfake is termed to define those images which are manipulated with the help of AI algorithms. Mainly, deepfakes are created using auto-encoders, generative adversarial networks (GANs) or face swap. As face swap technology seems to be continuously developing over time, the deepfakes generated by swaping faces will get only better. These manipulated images will be indistinguishable by human eye. So, in order to tackle the problem of anyone being mislead by these deepfakes, a system which can distinguish the manipulated images , i.e. face swapped images, from the real images is vital. To overcome this image forgery, customCNN model is implemented sequentially after collecting dataset, cropping out facial data from them and categorizing it into training, testing and validating images. Distinction of face swapped images from real images is performed by feeding the collected dataset into the customCNN model which is trained to detect real and synthetic faces.

Demo can be viewed in the DETECT section.
